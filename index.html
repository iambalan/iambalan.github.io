About Me

I am working as a Principal Data Scientist in Walmart Global Tech India. In my work I leverage GenAI, Agentic workflows and Software engineering to deliver data driven insights at scale at the world’s largest retailer.

With a PhD in AI-driven Web Personalization from IIT Madras, I bring deep research expertise to practical applications. My doctoral work explored novel approaches to understanding user behavior and delivering personalized experiences at scale.

Beyond industry work, I'm deeply passionate about education and knowledge sharing. Teaching is the ultimate test of understanding—as Einstein said, "If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough." I've taught 15+ courses across various institutes in India, serve as a core team member at IIT Madras Online Degree program, and review for top-tier publications including IEEE, ACM, Springer, and Elsevier.

In my free time, I love building AI projects, automating daily routines, and experimenting with new technologies. If you have cool ideas or want to collaborate—let's connect!
